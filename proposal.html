Project Proposal

I plan on creating a website featuring me, similar to a personal website featuring my professional portfolio. The objective of my website is for recruiters to get a deeper insight to my story:
  • About Me
  • Work Experience
  • Leadership Experience
  • Passion Projects
  • Skills
  • Interests

My target audience are recruiters from the tech industry who want a deeper look beyond my resume. I hope that my personal website will give a more well-rounded, unique perspective to me. My sitemap will include:
  1. Home
      2. This section will be the welcome page with my name and my career interest: Product Management and Program Management.
  2. About Me
      3. This section will have a quick introduction of me, from where I go to school and my overall passions and interests. It will follow the format of an interview "tell me about yourself," where I will also go into detail how I suit my intended career paths.
  3. Work Experience
      4. This section will be similar to a resume; however, it will combine my work experience and leadership experience. I may separate work experience from leadership experience. I haven't exactly decided yet.
  4. Personal Projects
      5. This section will include my self-started projects from this website, to other programming projects or personal programs, outside of work and Babson affiliation.
  5. Skills
      6. This section will include both my hard and soft skills. It can be separated into programming skills, technical skills, product management skills, project management skills, soft skills...etc.
  6. Contact Info
      7. This section will have my address, email, phone number, and social contacts. My social will have my LinkedIn, and possibly Github.

It will follow a full-scroll down site structure compared to completely separate pages.
